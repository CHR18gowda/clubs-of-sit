Cultural clubs

Drushya

The Drushya Club at Siddaganga Institute of Technology is a unique and dynamic student organization dedicated to the performing arts, specializing in drama, street plays, and mime, all performed in Kannada. As the only club at SIT with this focus, Drushya Club brings stories to life, raising awareness about social issues and celebrating Kannada culture. Members participate in intercollegiate competitions and community engagements, showcasing their skills and making a positive impact. Drushya Club provides a creative outlet, skill development opportunities, and a deeper appreciation for the local language and cultural heritage.

Arora

Aurora, the theatrical group of Siddaganga Institute of Technology, stands as a beacon of artistic expression and social activism. With the motto "We act for a cause, not for applause," Aurora epitomizes the fusion of creativity and conscience. Since its inception, Aurora has been dedicated to leveraging the power of theater to address pressing social issues and inspire positive change. Through thought-provoking performances, they delve into themes of justice, equality, and empathy, sparking conversations and fostering awareness within the community. Committed to excellence in both artistic endeavors and social impact, Aurora continues to captivate audiences while advocating for a more compassionate and inclusive society. Join us as we illuminate the stage with stories that resonate with the heart and challenge the mind.

Black Perl

The Black Perl Club, established in 2003 at Siddaganga Institute of Technology, is a prominent theatrical club known for its outstanding contributions to the performing arts. Specializing in various forms of theater, including stage plays, skits, and experimental performances, Black Perl has garnered widespread recognition for its creativity and talent. The club actively participates in intercollegiate competitions, consistently achieving success and winning numerous prizes. Through its productions, Black Perl aims to entertain, inspire, and provoke thought, leaving a lasting impact on its audience.

Panther

Panthers, the Siddaganga Institute of Technology's esteemed dance crew, has been igniting stages with rhythm and passion since its inception in 2006. With a rich legacy of dance excellence, Panthers embodies the spirit of creativity and dedication. Their tagline, 'If you have the passion to learn, we have a platform to awaken the dancer in you,' reflects their commitment to nurturing talent and fostering a vibrant dance community. From mesmerizing performances to innovative choreography, Panthers continuously pushes the boundaries of artistic expression. Through workshops, competitions, and collaborations, they inspire and empower dancers to shine brightly on every stage. With each graceful move and electrifying routine, Panthers celebrates the joy and transformative power of dance. Join the Panthers and embark on a journey of self-discovery, rhythm, and boundless creativity.

Distiny Music Clique

Destiny Music Clique, the official music band of Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur, has been mesmerizing audiences with its vibrant performances since 2016. Known for rocking the stage, this dynamic band seamlessly blends various musical genres, showcasing the exceptional talent and versatility of its members. With each performance, they bring a powerful mix of passion, energy, and musical excellence that leaves audiences in awe. Beyond the stage, Destiny Music Clique is a close-knit community of students united by their love for music. They have made a significant impact at college fests, intercollegiate competitions, and community events, earning accolades and a devoted fanbase. Their mission is to inspire and entertain through music, making every performance a memorable experience. Join Destiny Music Clique and be part of a musical journey that continues to set the stage on fire with unmatched enthusiasm and creativity.
